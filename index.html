The sustainability issue that we decided to tackle is behaviorial, since out of all the solutions we came up with, it is the most realistic.

First, we would begin by teaching everyone how to recycle and why it is important. Then we will let everyone know where recycling bins and other bins are located throughout the building.

Next, we will begin adding recycling bins in both the cafteria and locations where people hang out around in school.
