Paper Prototyping

Group Members: Aika, Tracy and Kevin

The sustainability issue that we decided to tackle is behaviorial, since it is the most realistic solution we have.

First, we would start by teaching everyone how to recycle and why it is important. Then we will let everyone know where recycling bins and other bins are throughout the building.

After teaching students about the importance of recycling, we would go talk with administrators and people in charge about buying more bins and placing them throughout locations in the school.

After getting the approval of administrators and others in charge, we would begin funding for the bins, through donations and possible funds from the alumni foundation.

We would also propose increasing passing time by 30 seconds or a minute to give more time for students to throw out their trash properly.

Next, we will begin adding recycling bins in both the cafeteria and locations where people hang out around in school.

After getting the bins, we will begin making signs to put around with the bins.
